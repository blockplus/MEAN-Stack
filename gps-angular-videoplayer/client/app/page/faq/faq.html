FAQs

1. What is MondayReplay?

MondayReplay is the home for your GoPro videos, created by GoPro users for other GoPro users, where it is possible for you to upload GoPro videos directly after having been on an adventure, as raw and unedited as you may like.

2. How it works?

On MondayReplay you can upload your videos from your Dropbox account, see videos of other users / friends upload and let you create highlights out of them. Cut, speed up, slow down, share and tag your and others͛videos, create your favorites͛ board.

3. What makes MondayReplay different from Vimeo or Youtube?

MondayReplay is not just a place to WATCH videos. It is a complete collaborative platform where our videos can be edited, shared and enjoyed by our friends or other Heroes. MondayReplay is a community for GoPro Heroes who want to share their whole adventures and create their own online where to live again the emotions of a GoPro feat with friends and likeminded users

4. What are the Uploads?

Uploads are the sessions uploaded by users. Uploads can contain as many files as you want and also your GPS track. You can find your Uploads in the section on your profile on MondayReplay. Don’t know how to upload?

5. What are the Highlights?

Highlights are short segments of videos cut out of Upload videos. You can create highlights out of your and others͛ Uploads using the player on the Upload page and tag them. You͛ll find them on your Highlights section on your profile where can be watched and commented by friends and users. Don’t know how to create highlights?

6. Is there an app?

Yes. Look for the MondayReplay Android app on the Play Store. Still there is not an iOS version available but won͛t be long

7. What are the Checkins?

When you are out on your outing, enable GPS and check-in in the app to broadcast your position, use check-ins to report conditions of terrain, wind, snow, water etc. or to be found by friends or others going for the same ride, checkins will show on your profile. Checkins give a record of what places peopleyou go, create the map of favorite spots and keep yourself updated on where your friends are.

8. Why should I sign in?

Signing in let you join a community of GoPro heroes you can share your ventures with, find suggestions, share contents and create your board of thrilling GoPro videos. If you have a Gopro MondayReplay lets you share your videos with the maximum speed and flexibility, if you haven͛t got a Gopro MondayReplay let you check what͛s going on and watch, remix and share what your friends and the community uplaod. From mobile you can stay in touch with your community wherever you are and check what spots are around you.

9. How do I sign in?

On MondayReplay you need to sign in with your Facebook account.

10. How do I upload my videos?

For your first upload you need to connect your MondayReplay account to your Dropbox account. Once done that, check your dropbox folder and you will see that a folder named MondayReplay has appeared in the Applications folder. In order to upload create a new folder in MondayReplay, the folder name will be the title, and drag&drop the video files from your GoPro Sd- card. They will be automatically transferred to your MondayReplay profile.

11. Can I upload everything I want?

MondayReplay is a platform for those who like filming and tracking their sport adventures with action cams. Anyway, you can upload whatever you want as far it is Mp4 video and GPX gps file. Nevertheless, MondayReplay has a moderation system and can remove users͛ contents which we do not deem appropriate.

12. How do I delete my account?

Contact us to delete your account. We'll proceed right away.

13. Which video sizes are supported?

You can upload all videos from action cams like GoPro, Sony, Garmin, Xiaomi and all the videos filmed with smartphone (all the actual mp4). For other sizes we suggest to try uploading, but we can͛t guarantee success.

14. Is there a limit of uploads?

You have no limit for your uploads! The only limit is the one of your Dropbox account, but remember: you can delete your Uploads from Dropbox once they are synchronized on MondayReplay, they will stay on your profile anyway.

15. How do I upload my GPS tracks?

You can upload your Gps tracks only uploading a GPX files along with your videos. We will show your track only in synch with the video in the Upload page. You can export a GPX file from pretty much any major Tracker website (Movescount, Garmin, Endomondo, Strava etc).

16. How do I synchronize the GPS with my video?

Add your .gpx file on Mondayreplay first, either along with the video or adding it to an existing upload. Go on the upload page, stop the video player on a frame with a spot you can recognize on the map. Unlock the synch button on the map, then move the cursor to the point on the map where you think is that spot and a box will appear asking: ͞Would you like to synchronize..?͟. Press ͚Yes͛ than play your video again to check if it works. If not, try to relocate your frame.

17. How do I delete my videos?

You need to go to the video you want to delete and click on the Delete button next to it.

18. Do I necessarily need a Dropbox account to upload my videos?

Yes, although MondayReplay is working on other ways to upload your videos easily.

19. Where do I have to upload my videos on Dropbox?

Once you connect your MondayReplay and Dropbox account a folder named MondayReplay will appear on your Dropbox in the Applications folder. You just need create another folder inside of it and start uploading here your videos. They will be automatically transferred to your MondayReplay profile.

20. How do I create highlights?

In the Upload page use the 7,15, 30 seconds buttons in the player to select the length and confirm, you have created an highlight in zero time. To understand better how to create highlights, go to the related page

21. Why should I create highlights from others͛ videos?

The Highlights you create can be commented on your timeline, can be shared to other sites, will be saved to your profile, will become visible to the community and your friends and contribute to create your own GoPro videos board on MondayReplay. I.e. There is a scene of you in one of your friends͛ video? Take it off from the whole upload, create your highlights video and publish it on your profile or share it where you want.

22. How do I create highlights of different length from the ones provided?

It is still not possible on MondayReplay, we are working on it.

23. Can I create a single highlights video including not consecutive parts of the same upload?

A 'playlist' function is coming up next. It will allow you to create your highlights from not consecutive parts of the same upload.

24. What's the difference between Uploads and Highlights?

The Uploads is the whole session the users put on Dropbox from their GoPro SD, that can have multiple files and be of any length. Highlights are the cuts and edits created by users out of the single uploads to givr evidence to just a small bit of the footage. Go to the Highlights or Upload session

25. Is MondayReplay a platform for video backups?

No, MondayReplay doesn͛t save the original dpo files after creating the streaming, you have to use another service for your master files.

26. Is MondayReplay a cloud storage platform for videos?

No, MondayReplay is a sharing service that keeps in memory the videos uploaded by users without saving the original files.

27. When can I remove my videos from Dropbox after uploading?

You can remove the videos once they are visible in the upload page and there are no more 'uploading files'.

28. Is it possible to upload files edited with GoPro Studio?

No, files transcoded by GoPro Studio won't work on MondayReplay. They have a GoPro proprietary codec which MondayReplay doesn't support.

29. What GPS format can I use?

By now MondayReplay supports only the .gpx format.
