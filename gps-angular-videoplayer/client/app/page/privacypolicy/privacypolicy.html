Privacy

Your privacy is important for us, we collect only the data necessary to the services we offer and we do so in full respect of international best practices and regulations

If you have any questions or concerns on this, you contact our customer service

Use of personal data

we collect data from your navigation like Ip address, username, time of access etc in order to grant you access to our site features.

Your registration on our site does not require you to disclose your name or other sensible information like address or contact. We also use third parties’ services like Facebook’s and in any case require only a working email for request verification. You can register anonymously.

Being a site for sharing video and geospatial information we obviously collect images and location information. While we offer tools to share this information with privacy, you should always assume that the default status of such information is public and even when it is communicated privately to friends the features of the site are such as your friends might share it with a different degree of privacy

Our mobile app might show your real-time location if you push it to your friends via a check-in’s

Data encryption and protection

all data sent to us is done via https encrypted connection, which is also used in the communication between our site and our databases. We use the widely accepted SSL (secure Socket Layer) technology

Cookies

We make use of cookies in order to optimise your visit to our site. Cookies are small text files that are saved in your browser (session cookies) or on your hard-disk (permanent cookies). We do not use cookies to run software on your computer

We use session cookies for login and authentication, these cookies are stored in your browser and deleted upon closing the browser

We use permanent cookies to save your settings and allow us a better personalisation of our services and a better experience for you on subsequent visits

We let also third-parties use their cookies, e.g. dropbox which is used for file upload or Facebook for authentication

Right of access and cancellation

You have the right to know what personal data of yours we possess and you might find tools on the site to know that, and modify or erase that data. For any other info about data retention please email us
