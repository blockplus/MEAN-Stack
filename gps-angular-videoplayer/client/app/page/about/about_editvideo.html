Customize your video







Upload your video and start editing: you can create highlights out of your session, speed up or slow down every single part of the video and track your position using the GPS.

Create your highlights

Become the video editor of your session by creating highlights:
- Select the duration of your highlights
- Move the cursor at the point you want them to start
- Click / Tap on ‘Create Highlights’ button
Your director’s cut will be available in the Sessions section right away!

GPS path tracking

Now that you uploaded the video, thanks to the GPS sync you’ll be able to browse the track you’ve been through in your adventure. Click on one of the pins on the map and the related video will play automatically, letting you live again those thrilling moments! Last but not least: you’ll have all the stats regarding speed and altitude for each second tracked on map!

Change the speed of your video

Speed up or slow down every single moment of your challenge to make it all even funnier!
